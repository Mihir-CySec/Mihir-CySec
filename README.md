## About Me

I think about risk the way a good auditor should — not as a checklist exercise, but as a genuine question: does this control actually work under real operating conditions, or does it just look good on paper?

Two years across enterprise IT audit, cloud security, and GRC consulting have reinforced one consistent finding: the gap between what policy documents say, what systems actually do, and what management believes is happening is where the real risk lives. Closing that gap, with evidence and without ambiguity, is the work I do.

Currently completing an MS in Cybersecurity at Northeastern University (GPA: 3.6) while consulting as a Technology Risk Advisor for a healthcare organization. Pursuing CISA certification. Seeking IT Audit, Technology Risk, and GRC roles at technology companies, Big 4 advisory firms, financial institutions, and start-ups
